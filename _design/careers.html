How we hire.

Our process is short, honest, and asks you to do real work — not whiteboard puzzles. We try to give a yes or no within two weeks of first contact, and we tell you why either way.

/ 01

Quick intro call

30 minutes. We learn what you've built and what you want to build next. You learn whether Sumora is the right shape for that.

/ 02

Take-home or portfolio

A small, time-boxed piece of real work — or a walkthrough of something you've already shipped. Always paid.

/ 03

Team conversations

Two to three deeper conversations with the people you'd actually work with — including a clinician, where relevant.

/ 04

Offer or honest no

If we're saying yes, we say so quickly. If we're saying no, you get the actual reason in writing. Either way, two weeks max.
